📡 Common APT Tradecraft

  • Spear-phishing with custom malware, document macros
  • Living-off-the-land (LOLBins, PowerShell, WMI)
  • Custom C2 protocols (HTTPS, DNS, email, satellite links)
  • Long-term persistence (registry, scheduled tasks, services)
  • Data exfiltration via encrypted channels (HTTPS, SMB, RDP)
